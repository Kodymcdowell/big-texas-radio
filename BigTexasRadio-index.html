Our Story

AMERICANA DONE RIGHT.

Big Texas Radio was built for music lovers who believe real music comes from real places. Founded and programmed by Angie Erwin out of Zavalla, Texas, we set out to create a home for the sounds that matter — Americana, Texas Country, Red Dirt, Blues, and everything in between.

We're not corporate radio. We're not algorithms. We're a passionate team of music lovers who hand-curate every playlist to bring you the authentic sound of Texas and the American heartland — streaming live to listeners across the country and around the world.

Whether you're driving down a country road, working in your shop, or just need something real — Big Texas Radio is your station.
